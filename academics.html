Services And Facilities

TRANSPORT.

Our School provides door-to-door transport services to over 80 % of it’s students who reside in greater Nairobi.
We manage a large fleet of 45 well-maintained buses and mini buses that service classified transport zones.
Each bus is driven by an experienced driver who is accompanied by trained assistant/ conductor who ensure the safe and timely travel of students

Services And Facilities

Libraries.

Our well-stocked libraries offer a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books, daily newspapers, periodicals, current journals, educational reports and audio visual aids. Reading and research are key elements that RGS fosters in developing an enquiring mind in our students.

Services And Facilities

Catering services.

Our Catering Department offers a wide range of nutritious, well balanced and delicious meals that meet the needs and tastes of students from kindergarten to high school level. We also cater to the needs of students on special diets. Each school has a team of teachers who supervise meal times to monitor service delivery and student satisfaction.

Vision & Mission

The School’s chief purpose is to develop the minds and consciences of its students so that they can become self-disciplined and resourceful, and therefore better able to serve society in a useful way. Above all, the School emphasizes academic excellence and moral integrity. The School aims at creating an environment that is conducive to learning; both experienced teachers and well-motivated students strive to create this environment.
